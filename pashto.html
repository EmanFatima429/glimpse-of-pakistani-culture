Explore Pakhtoon culture

The Pashto culture emphasizes values like honor, bravery, and loyalty. It also features distinctive clothing, such as shalwar kameez and Pakol hats, and a deep connection to tribal customs. Pashto cuisine includes flavorful dishes like Chapli Kebab and Peshawari naan.

Dance: The Attan, a traditional Pashtun dance, is performed during celebrations and social gatherings. It involves rhythmic movements and is accompanied by drums..
